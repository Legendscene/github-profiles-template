## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Dark theme**: The `theme=dark` matches the low-level aesthetic. Try `theme=codeSTACKr`, `theme=nightowl`, or `theme=react` for subtle variations.
2. **Benchmark section**: This is a unique conversation starter! Use real benchmarks from projects you've profiled or optimized. It shows you care about performance.
3. **Top languages config**: The `size_weight=0.5&count_weight=0.5` gives a more balanced view. Without these, small but frequent C files can dominate the chart.
4. **Crates.io badge**: If you publish Rust crates, link your crates.io profile. If you don't, link your personal site or blog about systems programming instead.
5. **Slice capsule header**: The `type=slice` creates a diagonal cut effect — unique among capsule types. Try `type=rect`, `type=wave`, or `type=cylinder` for different looks.
