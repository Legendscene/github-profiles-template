## рҹ’Ў Pro Tips

1. **The 3-color rotation is sacred** вҖ” The vaporwave aesthetic depends on exactly three colors alternating: pink (`#ff71ce`), cyan (`#01cdfe`), and purple (`#b967ff`). Always rotate in that order. Never use the same color twice in a row.
2. **Use full-width Unicode characters** вҖ” The subtitle and section headers use full-width characters (пҪҗпҪҒпҪ“пҪ“пҪүпҪҸпҪҺпҪҒпҪ”пҪ…). Use a full-width text converter online to turn any text into this style. It's essential for the vaporwave look.
3. **Keep the `bg_color=1a0b2e` on stats** вҖ” This deep purple-black background makes the stats cards match the vaporwave sunset palette. It's subtle but important вҖ” without it, the stats will have a white bg that breaks the aesthetic.
4. **Add a real vaporwave GIF** вҖ” Embed a GIF of a sunset grid, Greek statue, or VHS tape. Search Giphy for "vaporwave" or "aesthetic 80s". Use `width="80%"` to keep it responsive.
5. **Change the ASCII logo** вҖ” The "VAPORWAVE" ASCII was generated at [patorjk.com](https://patorjk.com/software/taag/). If you want your own word, use the "ANSI Regular" font for the same blocky style.
6. **MACINTOSH PLUS reference** вҖ” Add a subtle reference to the iconic vaporwave album "Floral Shoppe" by MACINTOSH PLUS. Use the album cover as a small image or reference the track "гғӘгӮөгғ•гғ©гғігӮҜ420 / зҸҫд»ЈгҒ®гӮігғігғ”гғҘгғј" in your music section.
7. **The lyrics section should be lo-fi** вҖ” Keep the lyrics section simple with 1-2 lines. It represents the "vaporwave stuck on repeat" feeling. Use actual song lyrics from a vaporwave/chillwave track.
