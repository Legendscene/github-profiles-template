## рҹ’Ў Pro Tips

1. **Keep the cyberpunk yellow (#ffeb3b) and pink (#ff0055)** вҖ” These two colors are the signature of the cyberpunk aesthetic. Don't change them unless you have a specific alternative palette. They contrast perfectly against the dark purple background (`#0d0015`).
2. **Use the "redical" theme for the activity graph** вҖ” It's a custom theme that gives a red/pink glow matching the cyberpunk look. If it doesn't load, fall back to `theme=radical`.
3. **Change the ASCII "HACK" logo** вҖ” The boxed ASCII art currently spells "HACK". Generate your own 5-letter word at [patorjk.com](https://patorjk.com/software/taag/) with the "ANSI Regular" font and paste it in.
4. **Add a glitch effect GIF** вҖ” Find a "glitch" or "static" GIF from Giphy and embed it in a `<details>` dropdown or as a small decoration for that authentic cyberpunk feel.
5. **Name your missions after cyberpunk quests** вҖ” Instead of boring project names, use lore-friendly names like "Neural Interface Protocol", "ICE Breaker v2", "Blackwall Bypass", "Soulkiller Mitigation".
6. **Dark stats background is critical** вҖ” The `bg_color=0d0015` on the stats cards is what makes them look like part of the cyberdeck. Don't remove it or the stats will have a white background that breaks the aesthetic.
7. **Make it look like a terminal** вҖ” Add `[BOOT SEQUENCE INITIALIZED]` or `[CONNECTION ESTABLISHED]` text at the top of your About section for extra immersion.
