## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **timeGradient magic**: The `color=timeGradient` capsule parameter creates a real-time rainbow gradient that shifts colors throughout the day. It's the single most powerful visual effect — always use `timeGradient` instead of static gradients for the holographic look.

2. **Transparent backgrounds**: The `bg_color=00000000` makes stats cards transparent, letting the rainbow capsule gradient show through. If your profile background is dark, the synthwave theme (pink/cyan) complements holographic perfectly.

3. **Prism divider**: Add extra rainbow dividers between sections using: `<img src="https://capsule-render.vercel.app/api?type=rect&color=timeGradient&height=2" width="100%" />`. This ties the theme together visually.

4. **Twinkling animation**: The `animation=twinkling` on the capsule header adds a subtle sparkle effect. For a more dramatic entrance, use `animation=fadeIn` instead — the text fades in like a hologram materializing.

5. **Instagram integration**: The Instagram link is perfect for this theme since you can showcase visual work. Add a follow badge: `<img src="https://img.shields.io/github/followers/yourusername?label=Followers&style=social&logo=instagram&color=ff6ec7" />` to match the theme.

6. **Holographic emoji palette**: Use ✦ ✧ ❖ ◈ ◆ as decorative elements. Replace the `✦` in the subtitle with `✧` or `❖` for variety. These Unicode characters render consistently across all devices and look like holographic sparkles.
