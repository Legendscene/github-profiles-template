## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Discord widget embed**: Replace the Discord badge with a live member count widget: `https://discord.com/api/guilds/{guild-id}/widget.json`. Shows real-time online count.

2. **Events as leverage**: List every event you've organized — even small ones. Volume of events signals experience more than individual size.

3. **Multi-community credibility**: The more communities you list, the more connected you appear. Always include communities where you hold leadership roles.

4. **Call to action**: The "JOIN OUR DISCORD" button is your most important element. Make sure the invite link never expires — set it to never expire in Discord settings.

5. **Meetup integration**: Link your Meetup group so people can RSVP directly from your GitHub profile. Great for growing your community.
