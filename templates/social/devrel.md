## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Talk count matters**: List every speaking engagement chronologically. Even small meetups signal community presence. Use years for easy scanning.

2. **Content diversity**: Show you create across formats — video, blog, podcast, docs. DevRel is about multi-channel communication.

3. **Conference URLs**: Link to actual conference pages or talk recordings (YouTube, SlideShare) for credibility. Dead links look bad.

4. **Tools you advocate**: Only list tools you've actually used in talks or content. Authenticity matters in DevRel — don't badge things you haven't shipped.

5. **Orange accent**: The `#FF8800` orange theme is warm and energetic. Keep all badges in warm tones for brand consistency.
