## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Prize emojis matter**: Use 🥇 🥈 🥉 for instant visual hierarchy. Recruiters scan these first. Always put your best win first.

2. **Devpost is mandatory**: Link every project to Devpost — it's the industry standard for hackathon validation. Empty Devpost looks suspicious.

3. **Group by year**: Sort by year descending (newest first). Shows momentum. If you won nothing this year, lead with last year.

4. **API sponsorships matter**: "Best Use of [X]" prizes signal sponsorships. List which APIs you've used — companies recruit from these.

5. **GitHub links for code**: Always link the GitHub repo alongside Devpost. Judges and recruiters want to see the actual code, not just the pitch.
