## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Checklist as progress**: Use `- [x]` for completed courses and `- [ ]` for in-progress. Shows growth trajectory to recruiters.

2. **Top languages context**: If most of your code is school assignments, the language stats will be accurate. For personal projects, use `&exclude_repo=course-repo1,course-repo2` to filter.

3. **Internship callout**: The "Open to internship opportunities" line signals recruiters. Change to "Open to full-time opportunities" as you approach graduation.

4. **GPA is optional**: If your GPA is below 3.0, omit it or use a different metric (Dean's List, course highlights).

5. **Project quality > quantity**: List 3 solid projects with descriptions rather than 10 trivial ones. Each should demonstrate different skills.
