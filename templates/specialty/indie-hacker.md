## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **MRR transparency**: Posting real MRR (even if small) builds trust. Start with whatever you have — $100/mo is honest and relatable.

2. **Product Hunt launch**: If you've launched on Product Hunt, link it and mention your rank. PH launches are indie hacker milestones.

3. **Building in public**: Tweet your progress daily and link your latest tweet in the "Building in Public" section. Shows active development.

4. **Emoji statuses**: Green dots = active revenue, yellow = building, blue = idea phase. This shows you ship, not just ideate.

5. **Failure count**: Listing failed products is a flex in indie hacker culture. It shows experience and resilience.
