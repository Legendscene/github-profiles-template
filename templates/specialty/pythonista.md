## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Live PyPI stats**: Use `![PyPI Downloads](https://img.shields.io/pypi/dm/{package})` for live download counts on each package.

2. **Version badge**: Add `![PyPI Version](https://img.shields.io/pypi/v/{package})` to show latest release version.

3. **Python theme**: The `github_dark` theme pairs perfectly with PyPI blue (#3776AB). Keep the blue accent consistent across badges.

4. **Top languages filter**: Python will dominate your top-langs if you write mostly Python. Add `&hide=html,css` to remove markup languages from the chart.

5. **PyPI profile**: Keep your PyPI profile updated with a bio and links back to your GitHub. It's a discovery vector for your packages.
