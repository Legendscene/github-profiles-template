## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Audit report links**: If your contracts have been audited, link the report: `<a href="https://github.com/yourusername/audit"><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Audited_by-00ff00?style=flat-square&logo=shield" /></a>`. Audited contracts = trust signal for users and employers.

2. **Gas dashboard**: Use a service like Tenderly or Dune Analytics to show live gas usage: `<a href="https://dashboard.tenderly.co/..."><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Gas_Dashboard-363636?style=flat-square" /></a>`. Concrete optimization numbers speak louder than claims.

3. **TVL badge**: If your DeFi protocol has Total Value Locked, add: `<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/TVL-$2.5M-3C3C3D?style=flat-square&logo=ethereum" />`. TVL is the #1 metric in DeFi.

4. **Hardhat profile**: Add your hardhat config highlights: `⚙️ Hardhat: Solidity 0.8.20, 1000+ tests, 99% coverage`. Shows engineering rigor beyond just writing contracts.

5. **Cross-chain deployments**: List all chains you've deployed to: `🌐 Deployed on: Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, BNB Chain`. Multi-chain experience is highly sought after.

6. **GitHub Copilot for Solidity**: Add a fun stat: `🤖 40% of Solidity code written with AI assistance — but every line reviewed manually`. Shows you leverage tools while maintaining quality.
