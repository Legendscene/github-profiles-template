## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Animate your avatar**: Instead of a static PNG, use `https://github.com/yourusername.png?size=200` — it auto-updates with your GitHub avatar. For animated effect, wrap with a pulsing CSS animation using `<img>` inside a `<div>` with `style`.

2. **Embed a Dribbble shot**: Use `<img src="https://cdn.dribbble.com/users/.../shots/.../attachments/.../file.png?compress=1&resize=400x300" />` to show live design previews directly in your profile — much more visual than text links.

3. **Color system**: Keep your badge colors consistent with your personal brand. Replace `BE50F6` (purple) in the typing SVG with your brand hex. Use a color palette of max 3 colors for badges.

4. **Case study links**: Instead of just "View", link each featured work to a detailed Behance/Dribbble case study with `?mode=case-study`. Recruiters love seeing the process, not just the final design.

5. **Figma embed**: Add an interactive Figma embed with `<iframe src="https://www.figma.com/embed?embed_host=github&url=https://www.figma.com/file/..." />` in a collapsed details/summary section for portfolio pieces.

6. **Availability status**: Add a custom badge showing availability: `<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Available_for_hire-be50f6?style=flat-square&logo=clockify&logoColor=white" />` — clients actively look for this signal.
