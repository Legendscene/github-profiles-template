## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Cost calculator embed**: Link to an AWS Pricing Calculator or Infracost output showing estimated monthly costs: `<a href="https://calculator.aws/#/estimate?id=..."><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Cost_Estimate-$2.4K_mo-FF9900?style=flat-square" /></a>`. Shows cost-consciousness — critical for architects.

2. **Well-Architected Framework**: Add badges for each pillar: `<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Well_Architected-FF9900?style=flat-square&logo=aws" />` — shows you care about best practices, not just spinning up resources.

3. **Multi-region diagram**: Replace the ASCII diagram with a text-based multi-region architecture (us-east-1, eu-west-1, ap-southeast-1) to demonstrate global-scale thinking.

4. **Migration experience**: Add a line: `🔄 Migrated: 200+ servers from on-prem → AWS in 6 months`. Quantified migration experience is what enterprises look for.

5. **Open source IaC**: Link to your Terraform modules registry: `<a href="https://registry.terraform.io/modules/yourusername"><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Terraform_Modules-7B42BC?style=flat-square&logo=terraform" /></a>`. Reusable modules = senior architect thinking.

6. **Incident management**: Add a section about past incidents: `⚡ Resolved: P0 outage affecting 500K users — root cause: misconfigured ALB idle timeout`. Real incident stories differentiate you.
