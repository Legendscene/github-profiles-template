## 🔧 Customization

| Variable | Replace with |
|----------|-------------|
| `your-name` | Your name |
| `yourusername` | Your GitHub username |
| `your-startup` | Your company |
| `yourstartup.com` | Your company website |
| `your-mission-statement` | Your mission |
| `industry`, `year`, etc. | Company details |
