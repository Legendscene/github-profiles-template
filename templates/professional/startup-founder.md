## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Investor-ready**: Add a link to your pitch deck or investor update using `<a href="https://pitch.com/yourdeck"><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Pitch_Deck-ff6b35?style=flat-square&logo=slides&logoColor=white" /></a>` in the social links row. Investors often check founder GitHub profiles.

2. **Highlight press**: Add a "Press" section with mentions using `<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/TechCrunch-ff6b35?style=flat-square&logo=techcrunch&logoColor=white" />` badges for media coverage — builds social proof.

3. **Product Hunt launch**: If you launched on Product Hunt, add a badge: `<a href="https://producthunt.com/posts/yourproduct"><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Product_Hunt-DA552F?style=flat-square&logo=producthunt&logoColor=white" /></a>` — it signals traction.

4. **Customer logos**: Use a table row of `<img src="..." width="80" />` with customer company logos (grayscale) to show who trusts you. White-label them using `?style=flat-square` shields.

5. **Revenue chart**: Embed a GitHub gist chart or use a `![Chart](https://quickchart.io/chart?...)` for a simple MRR growth line — investors love visual traction.

6. **Open source contributions**: Show your OSS cred by adding a pinned repo section: `<a href="https://github.com/yourusername/repo"><img src="https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api/pin/?username=yourusername&repo=repo&theme=default" /></a>`.
